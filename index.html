About Me

I was born in Queens, New York on July 20 1995. I lived in New York for nine years before my parents moved to Pennsylvania. I lived in Pennsylvania for nine years before I left. After I graduating from High School, I joined the United States Marine Corps, which I served for 6 years. After I retired from the Marine Corps, I went to diesel technology tech school for nine months. Once I graduated, I started working for Jagaur Land Rover Manhattan. I have worked for JLR since May of 2018.
